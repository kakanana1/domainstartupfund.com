A good name paves the path for a great startup.


So, what is the Domain Startup Fund all about? What are we trying to do here?

Consider that there have generally been three good ways to generate a great startup idea:
1. Solve a problem you're facing
2. Solve a problem someone else is facing
3. Carve out a new market entirely

But what if we're motivated, able-bodied, maybe know some other hungry able-bodied friends, but just don't have an idea in mind? Consider the 4th way, which is counter-intuitive, but often works EVEN better. And that way is to first scan a list of powerful brand names, perhaps on a marketplace like Brand Bucket or Squad Help, and come upon our business or startup that way...basically having a new idea fall in your lap...it's kinda like when you are shooting the shit with your friends and something someone says just hits the right note!
The Domain Startup Fund is ultimately just a great way to run HARD at a startup idea RISK-FREE for 1 year with the backing of a GREAT .com brand....the premise is how we do ONE thing, is how we do everything. If we pick a crappy name just because all the great .com's are taken, we sell our potential short.  The Domain Startup Fund opens you up to a world of great brand names at NO UPFRONT COST.

What are the terms of the Domain Startup Fund?

1% Equity in exchange for a great .COM
Nothing too formal. Just promise us you'll think of us when you've become the next Uber. Or even if you fall flat on your face.
Get 1 Year to Use the Domain Risk Free
It's your name from day 1. You get a valuable .com for nothing (well, maybe not exactly nothing!), simply because we believe in your vision and dreams. Take 1 year to launch your start-up into orbit. After 1 year, keep the domain for $2,500 USD outright or optionally $300/mo for 12 months if you want to continue with your dreams. Alternatively, return the domain name to us after 1 year if you don't feel like you want to pursue your idea any longer. 
Friendly Advice Along the Way
We live vicariously through your success, your ups and downs. You are our baby too and we'll support you with gentle hints and ideas here and there.
